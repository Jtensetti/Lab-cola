# Mixture A — “7X flavor”

## A1) 7X flavor oil (essential oil blend)

Age 1–2 days if possible.

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---:|
| Lemon oil | 45.8 mL |
| Lime oil | 36.5 mL |
| Orange oil | 1.2 mL |
| Tea tree oil | 8.0 mL |
| Cassia cinnamon oil | 4.5 mL |
| Nutmeg oil | 2.7 mL |
| Coriander oil | 0.7 mL |
| Fenchol | 0.6 mL |

**Yield:** ~100 mL flavor oil.

## A2) 7X alcohol solution

Dilute **20 mL** of the flavor oil to **1 L** total volume with **food-grade ethanol**.

This produces the “7X solution” used in final mixing.
