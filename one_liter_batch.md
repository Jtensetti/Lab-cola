# Final assembly — 1 liter batch

## Ingredients
- **Sugar:** 104 g (sucrose)
- **Water:** just enough to dissolve sugar into a syrup
- **Mixture B:** 10 mL (from `water_solution.md`)
- **7X alcohol solution:** 1 mL (from `7x_flavor.md`)  
  - Optional: adjust by a few extra drops to taste
- **Cold carbonated water:** to bring total volume to **1 L**

## Steps
1. Make a syrup: dissolve **104 g sugar** in a small amount of water.
2. Add flavor solutions to the syrup:
   - **10 mL** Mixture B
   - **1 mL** 7X alcohol solution
3. Heat the mixture until **nearly boiling**, then cover.  
4. Cool completely.
5. Dilute to **1 L** with cold carbonated water.
6. Optional: rest 12–24 h in a refrigerator for best flavor convergence.
